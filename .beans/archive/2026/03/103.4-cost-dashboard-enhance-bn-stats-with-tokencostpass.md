## What

Enhance `bn stats` to aggregate token usage, cost, and pass rates from RunRecord history across all beans. Currently stats only shows counts by status/priority.

## Why

From HN (politician): "What's your signal-to-token ratio?" The data is already stored in RunRecords — this is just a reporting view over it. Essential for understanding agent efficiency and identifying problem beans.

## Steps

1. Modify `src/commands/stats.rs` to load full beans (not just index) and aggregate RunRecord data:
   - Total tokens across all beans
   - Total cost across all beans
   - Average tokens per bean
   - First-attempt pass rate (beans that closed on attempt 1 / total closed)
   - Overall pass rate (beans closed within max_attempts / total attempted)
   - Most expensive bean (highest total tokens across all attempts)
   - Most retried bean (highest attempt count)
2. Add `--json` flag for machine-readable output
3. Add a "Cost" section to the stats output after the existing status/priority sections
